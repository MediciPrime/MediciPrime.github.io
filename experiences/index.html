Experiences

I have only recently entered the world of a Bioinformatician and I still have A LOT left to learn. However getting here hasn't been easy because I have made it a point to take the road less traveled. Now don't get me wrong, I don't consider myself as being superior to those who have choosen to follow the 'crowd'. I dislike being the center of attention and I haven't gone down this path because I wanted to be unique. I have already accepted the fact that as a male I have I have more of a chance at being a direct decendent of Genghis Khan than being unique.
You maybe asking yourself how? Well it's because as a male I share at least 99.5% of my DNA with other males. Now every male human has a 0.05% chance of being a direct decendent of Genghis Khan, so I likely have more of a chance at being a Mongal than being unique.
